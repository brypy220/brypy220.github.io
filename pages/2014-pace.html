[Spring Semester 2014]

"'You can sell money.'"

It's a shame I don't remember her name but...I'm certain she was the President of the Career Center. She helped me with my resume.

We were having a conversation about my goals to work on Wall Street, create a non-profit to help the kids in the city I came from

She told me I could sell money and that she had a brother who worked at a hedge fund if I was interested. I took that and ran with it and "knew" I was meant for sales.
