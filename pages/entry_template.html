Life Changing Event

First paragraph — set the scene. Describe the day, location, or your mindset at the time. Keep it natural, like you’re writing to yourself.

Second paragraph — expand on what happened, what you noticed, what you felt. Include small sensory details or emotional reactions.

Third paragraph — reflections, lessons, or realizations that came from this event. This is where you connect it to your larger journey.

Written from memory — reflections may be colored by time.
