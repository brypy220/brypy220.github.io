[Dec 28, 2013]

Kaboom

I had $2000, a 3.36 GPA, and an A- in six week Calc II course, I could obviously trade my money in the forex market for profit.

I was wrong and it wouldn't be the last time I was wrong. I blew up my account.

In hindsight, I should have been more patient with the trades. I was glad I failed, I needed the feedback. I learned weakness and strengths. It made me a better person because I learned that failure is lesson.

Written from memory — reflections may be colored by time.
